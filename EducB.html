Education & Background

My Country

My name is Gustavo and I come from the gorgeous land of Peru. A country full of unbelivable landscapes and a scrumptious variety of food. My favourites things about Peru are: Machu Picchu and Lomito Saltado.

Basic Education

I started my student life at the Santa Maria de Cervello school, a mainly religious school which taught me really valuable habits and knowledge. Sometimes I still miss my school a bit since I was there for 12 years.

College

Right after finishing highschool I started to prepare myself to apply for an Engineering career. The process ended successfully and finish the career in 5 years, I stood out mainly in automation, programming and engineering drawing. After finishing college I discovered what I was most passionate about: coding.

Languages

When I was in highschool I really enjoyed the process of learning english and I also got interested in learning german. Right now I can tell I'm at the B2 English level and the A2 German level.
