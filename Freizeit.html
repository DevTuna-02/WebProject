My Hobbies

Baking

Even though lockdown was a bit stressful at times, it helped me discover a hobby I enjoy very much: Baking. I really like baking bread-like recipes that use sourdough, I find it exciting to use a colony of random microorganisms to make food. The most difficult thing I have baked so far was Panettone, really tiring but worth it, a really unique combination of flavors there, you should try it ;).

Playing Guitar

Honestly I'm not that much into playing guitar, but it can be good entertainment at times. I just try to learn and play some melodies from songs I like, most of them rock or metal songs.

Videogames

Who hasn't some favourite videogame nowadays?. My personal favourites are StarCraft and Minecraft. The first one is a sci-fi RTS game with a really engaging history I had flying around my head day and night in my late childhood and youth. The second one is a cool sandbox game that I think I don't need to explain, but I already enjoyed "programming"there.

Anime

I guess everyone has already heard or read about anime at some point. I really enjoy some Anime genres, since they are really different from other kind of shows, they seem to not be from this world. I personally prefer comedy or thriller ones. My favourites are: Love is war , Full Metal Alchemist and Psycho Pass(which is a must) .
